‚ùì COMMON INTERVIEW QUESTIONS
----------------------------------------------------------------------

  BEHAVIORAL:
    1. Tell me about yourself
    2. What are your greatest strengths?
    3. What are your weaknesses?
    4. Why do you want to work here?
    5. Where do you see yourself in 5 years?

  TECHNICAL:
    1. Explain your most complex project
    2. How do you approach problem-solving?
    3. What's your development process?
    4. How do you ensure code quality?
    5. Describe your experience with [technology]

  SITUATIONAL:
    1. How would you handle a tight deadline?
    2. What would you do if you disagreed with your manager?
    3. How do you prioritize tasks?
    4. How would you handle an underperforming team member?
    5. What would you do if you made a mistake?

  COMPANY SPECIFIC:
    1. Why do you want to work at [Company]?
    2. What do you know about our company?
    3. How would you contribute to our team?
    4. What interests you about this role?
    5. How do your values align with ours?

ü§î QUESTIONS TO ASK THE INTERVIEWER
